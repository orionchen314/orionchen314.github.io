This project focuses on organizing the **first cycling race at Jilin University**, covering full-cycle event planning and execution.

**Scope**

- Event design and route planning
- Safety management and risk control
- Participant coordination and logistics

**Execution**

- Coordinated volunteers and race officials
- Implemented real-time event management during the race
- Ensured safety and smooth operation throughout the event

**Outcome**

- Successfully hosted the first large-scale cycling event on campus
- Built experience in managing complex, real-time systems involving people and logistics

**Key Insight**

Organizing a live event requires similar thinking to engineering systems: balancing constraints, ensuring reliability, and responding to dynamic conditions in real time.
