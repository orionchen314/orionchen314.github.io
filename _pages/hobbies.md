## Hobbies

Outside research, I enjoy several activities that help me stay curious, focused, and balanced.

### Cycling

I enjoy road cycling and long rides. It helps me build endurance and maintain a consistent training routine.

### Fitness Training

I regularly do strength training, with a focus on muscle gain, leg strength, and overall physical conditioning.

### Reading

I enjoy reading literature and nonfiction, especially works that combine philosophical reflection with strong narrative style.

### Technology and Coding

I like exploring programming, data analysis, and technical tools beyond my research work, especially when they can be applied to real experimental problems.

### Travel and Exploration

I enjoy discovering new places, planning trips, and learning through direct experience of different environments and cultures.
